About Hotel Santi

Welcome to Hotel Santi

Nestled in the stunning region of Nar, Manang, Nepal, Hotel Santi offers an unparalleled retreat at an altitude of over 4000 meters above sea level. Known as the Hidden Valley of Nepal, our hotel provides a unique opportunity to experience the tranquility and natural beauty of one of the most remote and breathtaking places on earth.

Our hotel is more than just a place to stay; it is an experience. With luxurious accommodations, unparalleled views, and warm hospitality, we aim to make your stay as memorable as possible. From the moment you arrive, you will be immersed in the serene atmosphere of the Himalayas, surrounded by pristine landscapes and traditional charm.

At Hotel Santi, we are committed to providing exceptional service and comfort. Our rooms are designed to offer the utmost in relaxation, with panoramic views of the majestic mountains and the picturesque village of Nar. Whether you are seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural enrichment, our hotel is the perfect base for exploring the Hidden Valley and beyond.

We invite you to experience the magic of Nar, Manang, and discover why Hotel Santi is the ideal destination for your Himalayan getaway.
